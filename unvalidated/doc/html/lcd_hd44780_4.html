lcd_hd44780_4

LCD library for HD44780 compatible LCDs, with 4 bits wide datatransfer

Author Rob Hamerling, Copyright (c) 2008..2008, all rights reserved.
Adapted-by Eur van Andel
Compiler >=2.4g

Description

Controls HD44780 compatible alphanumeric LCD screens.

Screens can range from 2x8 (2 lines, 8 chars) to 4x20.Uses 4 bit wide datapath + 2 handshake lines (total 6 PIC pins).Expects: - 2 pins for handshake: 'lcd_rs' and 'lcd_en'- a port nibble for data: 'data_port'
